cheesecakes:

Heat the oven to 160 degrees. Prepare the filling: mix the cheese, sugar and vanilla extract in a mixer until the sugar melts and a smooth mixture is obtained.
Add the eggs one by one and beat well. Add the sour cream as well, and beat again until smooth. Take the pan with the bottom out of the freezer, wrap the outside of the baking pan with aluminum foil, to make it more airtight, and pour the cheese filling
Place the mold inside another mold that is larger and contains comfortably and has high sides. Pour boiling water into the large pan up to the height of the sides of the pan with the cake. Bake in the oven for about 60 minutes, until the cake is firm on the sides and still vibrates in the center.
Take the cake out of the oven, and let it cool completely in the water in the bath. When the cake is cold, remove from the water bath and transfer to the refrigerator for 24 hours.

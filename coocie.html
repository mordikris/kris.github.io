chocolatechip cookies:

Heat the oven to 170 degrees. In a mixer bowl with a guitar hook (you can also use an electric hand mixer!) put butter, dark brown sugar, sugar, salt and vanilla and mix at high speed until you get a mixture with a soft creamy texture. Add an egg and continue mixing until it is incorporated into the mixture.
Add soda, baking powder and flour and mix until large lumps of dough are obtained. If using chocolate chips - add in and mix until the chocolate pieces are evenly distributed in the dough. If using regular chocolate - chop into rough pieces with a knife. Keep some of the chocolate aside.
Make 10-12 circles from the dough and place them at intervals on an oven tray lined with baking paper. Decorate the top with some more chocolate.
Bake for 12-17 minutes or until the cookies puff up and turn golden. If you make smaller cookies, the baking time is shortened.
Cool slightly at room temperature and serve.

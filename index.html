Yeast cake filled with halva:

2 English cake molds 30 cm long
For yeast dough:
500 grams (3 and a half cups) of flour 10 grams (2 teaspoons) dry yeast 100 grams of soft butter, cut into cubes (see notes for the fur version) 100 grams (1/2 cup) of sugar 2 L eggs (see notes for the vegan version) 180 ml (2/3 cup) milk (see notes for the non-fat version) Pinch of salt 1 teaspoon vanilla extract


For the filling of halva, almonds and white chocolate: 160 grams (2/3 cup) raw tahini 180 grams (1/2 cup) honey or silan 100 grams of natural roasted almonds, roughly chopped 150 grams of white chocolate, finely chopped For brushing:
1 beaten egg
For decoration: Sesame

Pistachio cake:

what is needed?
150 grams of pistachios ¼ 1 cups. 250 grams of sugar ¼ 1 cups. 3 eggs. 120 ml oil ½ cup. 100 grams of sour cream (or vegan yogurt). Zest from 1 whole lemon. ¼ teaspoon of rose water extract is optional, only for those who like it. 140 grams plain flour/whole wheat 1 cup. 1 teaspoon baking powder. 1 teaspoon of baking soda. ¼ teaspoon of salt for coating. 150 grams of powdered sugar. 2-5 tablespoons of sweet cream/milk/lemon juice (the cream will form a white coating). 2 drops of rose water extract is optional, only if you like. ¼ cup coarsely chopped pistachios.

Instructions: Heat an oven to 180 degrees non-turbo, grease an English cake pan and line it with baking paper. Put pistachios and sugar in a food processor and grind to a crumbly mixture. In a large bowl, Add sour cream, lemon zest and rose water and beat well. Add the mixture of pistachios and sugar and beat. Through a sieve/sieve add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Bake for 40-45 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out dry.

Amsterdamcookies:

Component:
100 grams of soft butter at room temperature (if you forgot to take it out of the refrigerator, heat the butter for about 10 seconds in the microwave until it is soft but not melted) ⅓ cup white sugar ⅓ cup compressed dark brown sugar 1 eggs (M or L) 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract 1 cup flour ¼ cup cocoa powder ½ teaspoon baking soda powder ½ teaspoon of salt 100 grams of milk chocolate (chocolate chips or chopped chocolate) About 200 grams of white chocolate broken into cubes (3 cubes per cookie)


Preparation: until the sugars are well absorbed in the butter and a smooth and slightly light mixture becomes. In the middle of mixing, stop the mixer and scrape the mixture from the sides of the bowl with a ladle. Add the egg and mix well until a uniform mixture is obtained. Add vanilla extract and mix. In a separate bowl, mix together all the dry ingredients: flour, cocoa, baking soda powder and salt. Spread baking paper on a work surface,

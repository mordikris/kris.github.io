Hamantaschen:

And just before starting two important tips:
Pay attention to baking - the cookies should remain relatively light and not brown. Try to process the dough as little as possible, to keep it crispy and melt in your mouth. Over-processing will yield hard manna ears. Quantity: 40-50 ears of manna
Ingredients for the manna ears: 200 grams of cold butter cut into cubes 2 full cups of flour (300 grams) 1/2 teaspoon baking powder 1 bag of powdered sugar (100 grams) 1 teaspoon vanilla extract ¼ teaspoon of salt ¼ to 1/3 cup milk
For the filling: Stuffing to taste (see suggestions below) For decoration: Powdered sugar cookies flower jam
